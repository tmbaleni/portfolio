The Process

Iteration is key

This methodology positions your digital experience as a living project that you should regulaly tweak and improve upon as you go.Bulding solutions in this way allows for continous improvements to your product and leads to the most effective and efficient results.

Research

The first and most critical step is understanding the problem we are trying to solve. From Who it affects? How? When? Where? and Why?.

Design

Includes final designs and a summary of each phase in the design process detailing why certain design decisions where made.

Development

From the approved designs the product goes through protoyping and testing to insure the solution meets the initial requirements.

Refine

Tweaks are made to improve various aspects of the product as more feedback is gathered before being deployed.
